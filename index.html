About

Hey, I'm Jennette, a seasoned product designer with a proven track record of building 0–1 products in dynamic startup environments. I lead with empathy-driven research, build alignment through storytelling, and prove value by shipping small and often.

My experience spans B2C SaaS with a deep focus on fintech. I've found my stride in demystifying financial workflows for underserved communities, empowering them with the tools and clarity to break free from financial burdens. More broadly, I'm driven by simplifying complex workflows and helping users get things done.

Currently, I'm passionate about discovering how to leverage LLMs across every part of the product-building process—and particularly interested in finding where AI can handle the heavy lifting, so designers can focus their energy on the work that matters most.
